MEJOR NINA

pick character game

Hello

This is a little game that I've created just for the laughs.

The point of it is to see whether your opinion about female anime characters is different than the one I have managed to obtain via Google Forms from my dear friends.

After submiting your vote, your screen will glow according to the answer you've given
(red - yours opinion is different; green - yours opinion is the same as theirs).

At the end you will also see your score and some flavor text.


Have fun!

Rules

  • Total number of rounds is 10
  • You can only choose one character
  • Everything is random - you will always have to choose between random characters
  • API used to load the images is kinda limited
  • I've tried to off set its limitation by adding delay between responses but it still might lock itself out if you keep refreshing the page
  • If you think you've chosen correctly and you are not satisfied with the background color - well, it's just a game
  • You can always play again but the randomness might not give you the same characters
